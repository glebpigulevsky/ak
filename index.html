Hi there!

My name is Gleb Pigulevsky and this is my personal page.

I am a frontend developer in an international company, an opensource contributor, and a teacher.

About me and my life.

Probably no one knows such a glorious city of Lida in which I was born and grew up. it is located in the western part of Belarus near the border with Lithuania. I also went to school there, where I got acquainted with programming for the first time. My first program was a script written in pseudocode based on C++ in the GameMaker game engine. It was back in 2003.

I also really like teaching people, no matter who, my son or an adult who needs to learn a new framework, I know how to find an approach to everyone

Then there was the university and the beginning of my career. for the most part, I was engaged in freelancing and, starting in 2019, I went into commercial projects.

In my free time, I like to travel by my own car with my family, I also love my home, but unfortunately, I have been there very rarely lately. I like to decorate it with all sorts of homemade interior items. My wife and I love making them with our own hands. I have two dogs that are like my children to me.

Chicken soup for Pups!

  • - 3 medium carrots
  • - 2 large onions
  • - 2 (3) chicken udo
  • - 4 medium potatoes
  • - A handful of vermicelli (you can't overdo it here, otherwise it will be chicken paste instead of soup).
  • - 1.5 cubes of seasoning for chicken soup

1. Put the washed chicken into boiling water
2. Cut the onion into small cubes, but not on a grater!
3. Grate the carrots on a coarse grater
4. First fry a little onion and then add carrots.
5. After getting a crust, pour a little water, a pinch of salt and sugar. Stew on low heat for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally and adding water.
6. Throw into the pot with chicken that stewed, plus potatoes cut into cubes, cook for another 10-15 minutes.
7. Take out the chicken and tear it with your hands.
8. Add cubes with seasoning and vermicelli at the end, cook for a couple more minutes until the vermicelli is not ready
9. Well and further salt and sugar under yourself, although usually 1.5 cubes is enough for your pot.
